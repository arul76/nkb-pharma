Our Products

Five evidence-based pharmaceutical products developed across critical therapeutic areas to address prevalent global health challenges.

TabletCardiovascular

CardioMax 10mg

Precision-formulated cardiovascular tablet containing a proprietary blend of CoQ10, Omega-3 fatty acids, and a patented vasodilatory compound.

Indications

  • Hypertension management
  • Hyperlipidemia
  • Post-cardiac event recovery
  • Preventive cardiovascular care

Dosage

One tablet daily with meals, or as prescribed by a healthcare professional.

Storage

Store below 25°C in a dry place, away from direct sunlight.

CapsuleNeurology

NeuroCare Plus

Neurological support capsule engineered to promote cognitive clarity, reduce neuroinflammation, and support the peripheral nervous system.

Indications

  • Cognitive decline support
  • Peripheral neuropathy
  • Memory enhancement
  • Stress-induced neural fatigue

Dosage

Two capsules twice daily after meals for optimal absorption.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place below 30°C. Keep sealed.

SyrupImmunology

ImmunShield

Comprehensive immunity booster syrup combining elderberry extract, zinc, vitamin C, echinacea, and selenium.

Indications

  • Recurrent infections
  • Post-illness recovery
  • Seasonal immunity boost
  • Chemotherapy support care

Dosage

10ml twice daily. Shake well before use. Refrigerate after opening.

Storage

Store between 2–8°C after opening. Use within 30 days.

SupplementOrthopedics

OsteoFlex

Next-generation bone and joint health supplement delivering bioavailable calcium carbonate, vitamin D3 (5000 IU), magnesium, and marine collagen peptides.

Indications

  • Osteoporosis prevention
  • Osteoarthritis management
  • Post-fracture bone healing
  • Athletic joint support

Dosage

Two tablets daily with water, preferably with a meal containing fats.

Storage

Store at room temperature, protected from moisture and light.

SolutionRespiratory

RespiClear

Advanced respiratory health solution combining N-acetylcysteine, bromhexine, and eucalyptus essential oil complex.

Indications

  • Chronic bronchitis
  • COPD management
  • Asthma-related mucus clearance
  • Post-respiratory infection recovery

Dosage

5ml in 200ml warm water, inhaled or ingested as directed by physician.

Storage

Keep in a cool, ventilated area below 25°C. Avoid freezing.
